====== Shepelah ======

===== Definition: =====
The lowland region of Israel east of Jerusalem. 

  * The Shepelah was known for being the eastern foothills oh the mountains around Jerusalem. 
  * Receiving a moist breeze from the Mediterranean Sea the Shepelah was the typical place to grave sheep and goats.
