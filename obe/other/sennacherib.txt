====== Sennacherib ======

===== Facts: =====
A powerful king of Assyria. 

  * He is known for his wars against Babylon and the kingdom of Judah. 
  * In the Bible the books of 1 and 2 Kings  and 1 and 2 Chronicles records his reign. 
  * He is known for making the city of Nineveh great.
