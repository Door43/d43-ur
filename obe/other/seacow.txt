====== Sea cow ======

===== Definition: =====
A large ocean dwelling animal. 

  * Thought to resemble a swimming cow or ox.
  * Sometimes their skin was used to write on. 

(See also: [[:En:obe:other:cow]], [[:En:obe:other:ox]])
