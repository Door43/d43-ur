====== thorn, thistle ======

===== Facts: =====
A hard, sharp growth on a plant. 

  * Part of the fall when sin came into the world is that the group would produce thorn and thistles causing growing crops to be more difficult. 
  * Sometimes in the Bible "thorn" or "thistle" is used as a metaphor for annoyance or constant temptation. 
  * There was a crown of thorn placed on Jesus' head before he was crucified.
