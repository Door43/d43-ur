====== storehouse ======

===== Definition: =====
A "storehouse" is a building, usually large, used for storing and saving food or other goods.

  * In the Bible a "storehouse" was usually used to save food for when a famine would occur. 
  * A "storehouse" was sometimes a way of building wealth in a family. 

(See also: [[:En:obe:other:famine]])
