====== prostrate ======

===== Definition: =====
Prostrate means to lay face down stretched out on the ground. 

  * Laying on the ground prostrate was an important part of worshiping. 
  * This was a common way to pray in the Bible. 
  * "To prostrate oneself" is an action as well as a position.
