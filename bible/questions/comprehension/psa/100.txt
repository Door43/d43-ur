====== Psalms Chapter 100 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? Who is the writer inviting to shout joyfully to Yahweh?

A. "Shout joyfully to Yahweh, all the earth." [100:1]

Q? What are the two ways the earth is told to serve Yahweh?

A. "Serve Yahweh with gladness; come before his presence with joyful singing." [100:2]

Q? Why does the writer say that we belong to Yahweh?

A. "Yahweh is God; he made us, and we are his." [100:3]

Q? When the writer says that Yahweh is good, what is it that he says endures forever?

A. "Yahweh is good; his covenant faithfulness endures forever." [100:5]
