====== Psalms Chapter 046 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? How does the psalmist describe God?

A. God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble. [46:1]

Q? What will they not do, even though the earth should change and the mountains should be shaken into the heart of the seas?

A. Therefore, they will not fear. [46:2]

Q? What is there in the city of God, which the streams of a river make happy?

A. There is the holy place of the tabernacles of the Most High. [46:4]

Q? What will the city of God not do?

A. The city will not be moved. [46:5]

Q? What will God do for the city?

A. God will help the city, and he will do so at the dawn of the morning. [46:5]

Q? What happened to the earth when God lifted up his voice after the nations raged and the kingdoms were shaken?

A. The earth melted. [46:6]

Q? Where and what is Yahweh of hosts, the God of Jacob?

A. Yahweh of hosts is with them; the God of Jacob is their refuge. [46:7]

Q? What does Yahweh do to wars?

A. He makes wars cease to the ends of the earth, and he breaks the bow, cuts the spear into pieces, and burns up the shields. [46:9]

Q? What does God tell the people?

A. Be quiet and know that he is God. He will be exalted among the nations and on the earth. [46:10]

Q? Who is with the people?

A. Yahweh of hosts is with us. [46:11]
