====== Psalms Chapter 067 ======

===== Comprehension Questions and Answers =====

These questions will be used by translators to conduct community checks of this chapter.

Q? What is the writer asking of God and why?

A. The writer is asking God to be merciful, bless and cause his face to shine on them so that God's ways and salvation may be known among all nations. [67:1,2]

Q? Who should praise God?

A. The writer says that all the peoples should praise God. [67:3]

Q? Why should the nations be glad and sing?

A. The nations should be glad and sing because God will judge the peoples with justice and govern the nations. [67:4]

Q? Why should the peoples praise God?

A. The people should praise God because God has blessed them with the earth's harvest. [67:5,6,7]
