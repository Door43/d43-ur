===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.

  * **bring his sons** - "bring Aaron's sons"
  * **coats...sashes...headbands** - See how you translated this in [[ur:bible:notes:exo:28:40|28:40]].
  * **The work of the priesthood** - "the duty of being priests"
  * **will belong to them** - AT: "will belong to them and their descendants" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **permanent law** - See how you translated this in [[ur:bible:notes:exo:28:42|28:43]].
