===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Lehi **- Translate this place the same way you translated it in [[:ur:bible:notes:jdg:15:09|15:9]].
  * **Yahweh's Spirit came on him in power**  - The phrase "came on him" refers to help that Yahweh gave Samson. AT: "Yahweh's spirit added powerful strength to Samson" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom|Idiom]])
  * **ropes on his arms became like burnt flax**  - The ropes are compared to burnt flax because they were very easy for Samson to break. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **flax**  - fibers from the flax plant used for making threads and cloth
