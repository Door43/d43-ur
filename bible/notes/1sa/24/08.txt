===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **and did respect to him** - "to display respect for Saul"
    * **Why do you listen to the men who say, 'See, David is seeking your harm?** - A rhetorical question containing the desired response.  AT: "You should not listen to the men who say, 'See, David is seeking your harm." (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
