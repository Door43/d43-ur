===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You must explain** - Yahweh is speaking to Moses.
  * **must carry his own guilt**  - "is responsible for his guilt" or "the people must punish him" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **must surely be put to death** - AT: "The people must surely put him to death" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
