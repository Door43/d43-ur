===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

  * **The one who is bent down**  - This refers to the people of Israel who are prisoners and forced to work for the Babylonians.
  * **nor will he lack bread**  - AT: "nor will he lack food"
  * **Yahweh of hosts**  - See how you translated this in [[:ur:bible:notes:isa:01:09|1:9]].
