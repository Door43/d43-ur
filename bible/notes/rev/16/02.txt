===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **poured out his bowl** - AT: "poured out the wine in his bowl" or "poured out God's wrath which was in his bowl" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **painful sores** - "painful wounds." These could be infections from diseases or injuries that have not healed.
  * **mark of the beast** - See how you translated this in [[ur:bible:notes:rev:13:15|13:17]].
