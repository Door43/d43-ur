===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **ten slave wives**  - A number referring to an amount (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
    * **under guard**  - An expression describing the condition of being watched or protected by an official attendant.
    * **He provided**  -  refers to David (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])  
    * **their needs**  -  refers to his slave wives. (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns]]) 
    * **widows**  - women who have lost their husbands through death
