  * **these**  - Refers to the following words continuing through verse seven as an introduction. (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns]])

  * **I, me**  - Refer to David. (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
