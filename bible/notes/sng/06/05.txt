===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **overwhelm** - "overcome," "affect me greatly," or "affect me very strongly"
  * ** Your hair is like a flock of goats ** - He describes her hair as an object that he finds beautiful. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **the slopes of Mount Gilead** - "the sides of Mount Gilead" or "the rising ground of Mount Gilead"
