===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **flowers have appeared** - "flowers have come into sight"
    * **pruning**  - cutting off branches on a bush so that it will produce more fruit or look better 
    * **the sound of the doves** - Doves are known for their soft coo sound.
    * **vines are in blossom** - "vines are flowering" or "vines have flowers"
    * **fragrance** - "sweet smell"
