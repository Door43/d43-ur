===== Translation Notes: =====

This continues to list Solomon's Officials.

  * **Ahimaaz** - He was from the tribe of Naphtali and married Basemath. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Baana** - His father, Hushai was from the tribe of Asher  and married Bealoth. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **Jehoshaphat** - His father, Paruah, was from the tribe of Issachar. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
