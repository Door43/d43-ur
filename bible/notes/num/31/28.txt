===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then levy a tax to be given to me **-**** AT: "The soldiers who were in battle must give me some of their plunder."
  * **levy a tax **- This means an amount of something of value that is to be paid or collected to cover a specific tax.
  * **Take this tax from their half **  - "Their" is specifically refering to the tax on the soldier's plunder.
