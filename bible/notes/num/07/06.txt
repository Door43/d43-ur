===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **two carts and four oxen**  - AT: "2 carts and 4 oxen" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **because of what their work needed**  - AT: "because this is what they needed to do their work"
  * **four carts and eight oxen**  - AT: "4 carts and 8 oxen" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers|Translate Numbers]])
  * **in the care of Ithamar son of Aaron the priest**  - This means that Ithamar showed the descendants of Merari what to do as they worked.
