===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the priests who were anointed and who were ordained** - AT: "the priests whom Moses anointed and ordained" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **fell dead before Yahweh** - AT: "died suddenly in the presence of Yahweh" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **they offered to him unacceptable fire** - AT: "they burned an incense offering to Yahweh in an improper way" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
