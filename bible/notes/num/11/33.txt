===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **That place was named **- AT: "They named that place" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Kibroth Hattaavah **- This was a burial place for those who lusted or desired more of the quail.
  * **craved meat**  - AT: "a strong desire for meat"
  * **Hazeroth**  - This was a place where nomads stayed.
  * **While the meat was still between their teeth, while they were chewing it, Yahweh became angry at them**  - 'They' refers to those of the Israelites who craved meat.
