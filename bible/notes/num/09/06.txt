===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **by the body of a dead man** - "by touching the body of a dead man"
  * **said to him** - Here the word "him" refers to Moses.
  * **Why do you keep us away from offering the sacrifice...among the people of Israel?** - The men ask this question in order to complain that they are not allowed to participate in celebrating the Passover. AT: "It is not fair that you keep us away from offering the sacrifice...among the people of Israel." (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
