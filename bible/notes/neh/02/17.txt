===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You see the trouble** - Here "you" is plural, referring to all the people mentioned in [[ur:bible:notes:neh:02:15|02:16]].
  * **so we will no longer be in disgrace** - "so we will end this disgrace." 
  * **I told them** - "Then I said to them"
  * **the good hand of my God was on me** - Here "good hand" is a metonym for God's presence or power.  AT: "the powerful help of God was upon me" or "God was with me" (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **Let us rise up and build** - The phrase "rise up and build" is an idiom. AT: "Let us begin building." (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **So they strengthened their hands for the good work** - "So they got ready to go to work" or "so they encouraged themselves to do the good work."
