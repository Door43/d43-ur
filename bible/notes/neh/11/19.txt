===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gatekeepers**  - See how you translated this in [[:ur:bible:notes:neh:07:01|7:1]]
  * **Akkub...Talmon...Ziha...Gishpa** - These are male names.  (See [[ur:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **their associates** - "their brothers"
  * **172 men** - See how you translated numbers in [[ur:bible:notes:neh:07:11|07:11-14]].
  * **The temple workers lived in Ophel, and Ziha and Gishpa were in charge of them**- "The temple workers lived in Ophel. Ziha and Gishpa were over the temple workers."
  * **Ophel** - This is the name of a place. (See: [[ur:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
