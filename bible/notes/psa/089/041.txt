===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **All who pass by** - He feels like everyone who passed by has robbed him. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
  * **raised the right hand of his enemies** - To "raise the right hand" means that Yahweh adds to the strength and control of his enemies. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **turn back the edge of the sword** - Yahweh weakens his efforts in battle. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
