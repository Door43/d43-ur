===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **call to mind** - The writer asks Yahweh to bring this up in his memory. AT: "remember" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **bear in my heart** - AT: "carry the burden in my feelings" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **hurl**  - This means to throw violently. Here to say or shout something loudly, such as the insults of the enemies. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **the footsteps** - The writer compares the steps of the king to the choices and movements he makes in governing the nation. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
