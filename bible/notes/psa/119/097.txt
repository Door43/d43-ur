===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **MEM:**  This is the thirteenth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 97-104 begins with this letter.
  * **your commandments are always with me**  - AT: "your commandments are always on my mind" or "I frequently think about your commandments" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification|Personification]] and [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole|Hyperbole]])
