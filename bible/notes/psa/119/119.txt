===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **You remove all of the wicked of the earth like slag**  - Slag is the waste or undesired leftovers in the process of refining gold or other metals. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile|Simile]])
  * **solemn commands**  - See how you translated this in [[:ur:bible:notes:psa:025:010|25:10]].
  * **trembles in fear of you**  - AT: "shakes because I fear you"
