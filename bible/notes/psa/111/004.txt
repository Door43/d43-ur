===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **gracious and merciful** - These two words have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **call to mind**  - AT: "remember" (See: [[ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **his powerful works** - AT: "his power" (See: [[ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
  * **inheritance of the nations** - This is a reference to the land of other nations. This land would have normally been inherited.
