===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **your ways ... your paths** - "Ways" and "paths" represent behavior. "The phrases "your ways" and "your paths" refer to the way God wants people to behave. (See [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **I hope in you** - "I depend on you" or "I wait patiently for you"
