===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I do not associate with** - "I do not keep company with" or "I do not sit with"
  * **nor do I mingle with** - "and I do not join with" 
  * **dishonest people** - "people who lie"
  * **evildoers** - "people who do what is evil"
