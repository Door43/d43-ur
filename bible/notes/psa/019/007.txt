===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **the soul ... the heart ... the eyes** - All three of these words refer to the whole person. AT: "a person" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **are reliable** - "are stable"
    * **to the inexperienced** - AT: "to those who have no experience"
    * **giving insight to the eyes** - "making the eyes bright" or "causing the eyes to shine" AT: "giving understanding to the eyes"
