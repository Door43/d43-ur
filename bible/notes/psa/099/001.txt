===== Translation Notes: =====

See: [[:ur:ta:workbench:translate:poetry|Translating Poetry]] and [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]]

  * **tremble** - shake with fear
  * **He sits enthroned above the cherubim** - AT: "Yahweh rules above the cherubim"
  * **quakes** - shakes
  * **he is exalted** - "People exalt him" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) 
  * **Let them praise your great and awesome name**  - Here the author shifts from speaking about God, to speaking to God. After this phrase though, he shifts back to speaking about God. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
