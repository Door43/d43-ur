===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I will make your name** - The word "I" refers to the author. The word "your" refers to God.
  * **forever and ever** - These words have the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
