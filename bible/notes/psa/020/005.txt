===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **we will rejoice in your salvation** - Possible meanings are 1) the people will rejoice in the salvation that the king will experience 2) the people and the king together will rejoice in the salvation that God will give 3) the people and the king will rejoice together in the salvation that the king will experience. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]] or [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
  * **in the name of our God** - AT: "in honor of our God" or "for the reputation of our God" or "for our God" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
  * **we will raise banners** - "we will raise flags" or "we will lift up banners"
  * **Now** - This word is used here to mark a break in the psalm. Here another person takes over.
  * **I know** - The word "I" probably refers to the king who is speaking in this section.
  * **his anointed** - The king is speaking about himself in the third person, possibly to show that this will be true for who will be king after he dies. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]])
  * **grant all your petitions** - "give you all your requests" or "award you with everything you asked for." The word "your" refers to the king.
  * **the saving strength of his right hand** - AT: "his saving strength" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
