===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **DO NOT WRITE NOTES HERE - SAVE FOR GRETCHEN'S TEAM GS**
  * **I will appoint...**  - God is speaking. Nathan will give this message to King David.
  * "I" refers to God.
  * "You" refers to David.
  * **will plant them there**  - "will move and settle the Israelites in their own land"
  * **commanded judges to be over my people Israel**  - Before King Saul and King David, the Israelites were lead by judges not kings.
  * **Moreover I tell you that I, Yahweh, will build you a house.**  - AT: "Therefore, I tell you that I, God, will establish your kingdom that will continue to exist after you die."
