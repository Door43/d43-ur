===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Hiram** - This is the name of the king. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
  * **carpenters** - people whose job is to make things with wood
  * **masons** - people whose job is to make things with stone or brick
  * **established him as** - AT: "made him"
  * **his kingdom was exalted on high for the sake of his people Israel** - AT: "Yahweh gave honor to David's kingdom to help the people of Israel" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
