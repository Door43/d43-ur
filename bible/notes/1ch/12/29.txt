  * **From Benjamin, Saul's tribe** - AT: "From Saul's tribe, the tribe of Benjamin"
  * **3,000** - "three thousand men" (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])

  * **20,800** - "twenty thousand eight hundred"
  * **18,000** - "eighteen thousand"
