===== Translation Notes: =====

Paul continues his discussion with the new believers in Ephesus.

  * **Into what then were you baptized? ** - "Whose baptism were you baptized in?" or "Under whose name were you baptized?"
  * **They said ** - "the disciples said"
  * **Into John’s baptism ** - "With the baptism of John."
  * **the baptism of repentance** - "the baptism that people requested when they wanted to turn away from their sin".
  * **come after him** - "come after John the Baptist in time" and not following after him physically.
