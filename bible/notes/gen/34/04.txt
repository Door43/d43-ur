===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Now Jacob **- "Now" is used here to mark a change from the story to background information about Jacob.(See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background|Writing Styles - Background Information]])
  * **Jacob heard that he**  - The word "he" refers to Shechem.
  * **he had defiled **- Jacob heard that Shechem raped Dinah. "Defiled" can be translated as "dishonored" or "disgraced." (UDB)
  * **held his peace**  - "kept silent"
