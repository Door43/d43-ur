===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh is speaking about what will happen to Elam.

  * **bowmen**  - "man who is skilled with a bow and arrow".
  * **four winds from the four corners**  - (See: Numbers) "4 winds from the 4 corners".
  * **four corners of**  - AT: "all directions, everywhere". (See: Idiom)
  * **those scattered**  - AT: "the outcasts"
