===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Is this what makes you a good king**  - Yahweh is asking a rhetorical question to the kings of Judah to correct them because they forced people to build great palaces for them, but did not pay them. (See : [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Did not your father also eat and drink**  - Yahweh is referring to King Jehoiakim's father King Josiah to emphasize that he enjoyed his life but was just and righteous. (See : [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion|Rhetorical Question]])
  * **Is this not what it means to know me?**  - AT: "Yahweh says, this is what it means to know me."
  * **this is Yahweh’s declaration**  - "this is what Yahweh has declared"
