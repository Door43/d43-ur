===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Then Jeremiah said to all the people—to the men and women, and all the people who answered him—he proclaimed and said** - AT:  "Then Jeremiah said to all the people, both men and women, and all who answered him, saying"
  * **Did not Yahweh remember ... ?** - This is the beginning of a rhetorical question, where Jeremiah is asking the people to remember how good it is to burn incense to honor Yahweh. AT: "Yahweh remembered ... " (See: [[ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
  * **Did not Yahweh remember the incense that you burned in the cities of Judah and the streets of Jerusalem—you and your ancestors, your kings and leaders, and the people of the land?** - Jeremiah agrees that they did as they said but in retort Jeremiah asks, 'Did God not repay their idolatry?' (See: [[ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
