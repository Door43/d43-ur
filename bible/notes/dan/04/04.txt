===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **I was living happily ... in my palace** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing. (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **But a dream I had ... my mind troubled me** - Both "dream" and "visions" are parallel because they mean the same thing. Also, "afraid" and "troubled me" are parallel. (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
