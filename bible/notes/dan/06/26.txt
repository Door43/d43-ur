===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **tremble**  - shake with fear
  * **he is the living God and lives forever**  - The two phrases "the living God" and "lives forever" repeat the same concept, that God lives forever. (See: ([[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **and his kingdom will … his dominion will…**  - These two phrases are parallel, emphasizing how God's kingdom will never end. (See: ([[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism|Parallelism]])
  * **his dominion will be to the end**  - "he will rule forever"
