===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **to the rebuke of the wise** - AT: "when wise people rebuke you" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
  * **to listen to the song of fools** - AT: "to listen to fools sing"
  * **like the crackling of thorns burning under a pot so also is the laughter of fools** - The "laughter of fools" is compared to burning thorns that are loud but quickly burn out. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
