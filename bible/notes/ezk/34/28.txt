===== Translation Notes: =====

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Israel.

  * **plunder** - See how you translated "plunder" in [[ur:bible:notes:ezk:07:20|7:21]].
  * **a peaceful planting place** - Possible meanings are 1) "a garden where they can be in peace" or 2) "a garden that will be famous"
  * **starve with hunger** - AT: "starve " or "starve because of lack of food"  (See: [[ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
