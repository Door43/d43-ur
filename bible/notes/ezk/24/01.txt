===== Translation Notes: =====
Yahweh speaks to Ezekiel.
  * **the word of Yahweh came to me** - AT: "Yahweh spoke to me"
  * **the king of Babylon has besieged Jerusalem** - The army of Babylon is referred to by its leader. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
