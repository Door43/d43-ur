===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **the word of Yahweh came to me and said**  - See how you translated this in [[:ur:bible:notes:ezk:03:16|3:16]].
  * **Son of man**  - Ezekiel (me)
  * **prophesying out of their own minds**  - prophesying from their own thoughts
  * **follow their own spirit**  - This phrase is used to refer to the prophets doing what they want.  The word "spirit" refers to their thoughts and the word "follow" refers to what they do. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **Thus says the Lord Yahweh **- See how you translated this in [[:ur:bible:notes:ezk:05:05|5:5]].
  * **jackals**- wild animals like dogs or a small wolves.
  * **like jackals in the wastelands**  - Like jackals that steal food from other animals in the desert, the prophets steal from the people of Israel when they lie about having messages from God. (See: [[ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
