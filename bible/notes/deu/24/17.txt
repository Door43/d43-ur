===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **You must not force away the justice that is due the foreigner...**  - AT: "You must see to it that foreigners receive just treatment." (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **cloak**  - This is a large rectangular piece of cloth, which the person can wrap around himself to keep warm at night. See [[ur:bible:notes:deu:24:12]].
    * **call to mind** - "remember".
