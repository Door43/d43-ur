===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

    * **Only pay attention to yourself and carefully guard keep your soul** - AT: "Pay careful attention and be sure to keep these things in your minds always" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
    * **do not forget...do not leave your heart** - These phrases means the same thing and they are combined to emphasize that the people of Israel must remember what they have seen. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]] and [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
    * **your eyes have seen** - AT: "you have seen" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **Assemble me the people** - "Bring the people to me"
