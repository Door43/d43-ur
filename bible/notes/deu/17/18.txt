===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **so as to keep all the words of this law and these statutes, to observe them** - The words "to keep" and "to observe" mean the same thing. They emphasize that the king must obey all of God's law. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
