===== Translation Notes: =====
Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
  * **like a devouring fire** - This emphasizes God's power and his ability to destroy the armies of the other nations. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
  * **he will bring them down before you** - "he will humble them in front of you" or "he will subdue them in front of you"
