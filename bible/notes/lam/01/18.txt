===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **I have rebelled** - Here Jerusalem begins to speak again, as if it were a woman. (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_123person]] and [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
    * **see my sorrow** - AT: "see how extremely sad I am" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
    * **My virgins and my vigorous men** - AT: "so many of my people" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
    * **vigorous men** - See how you translated this in [[:ur:bible:notes:lam:01:15|1:15]].
    * **they were treacherous towards me** - AT: "they betrayed me"
    * **to restore their lives** - AT: “to save their lives” or “to restore their stength”
