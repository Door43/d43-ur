===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **They have pursued us** - Possible meanings are 1) "It is as though those who pursue us have put their feet on our necks" (UDB), or 2) "They have pursued us very closely," or 3) "They have pursued us and put a yoke on our necks."
    * **They** - This refers to the Babylon army.
    * **reached out with our hands to** - AT: "made a treaty with" or "surrendered to" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
    * **to be filled with food** - AT: "so that we would have food to eat"
    * **they are no more** - AT: "they have died" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
    * **we bear their sins** - AT: "we bear the punishment for their sins" (See: [[:ur:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
